Case Study

Data & Analytics Infrastructure

Ingestion, normalization, storage, query, and analysis workflows using PostgreSQL, ClickHouse, SQLite, Python, SQL, event-based data models, replayable logs, and performance-oriented analytics.

Problem

Data-heavy workflows needed reliable ingestion, local and analytical storage, repeatable experiment tracking, and query paths that made comparison and debugging practical at higher volumes.

What I Built

  • Market and event-data ingestion pipelines with normalization and replayable storage.
  • SQLite-backed local research workflows for portable inspection and iteration.
  • ClickHouse-backed vectorized backtesting and high-volume strategy evaluation paths.
  • PostgreSQL-style runtime/job tracking models for operational workflows.
  • Sortable comparison views for strategy, experiment, and performance analysis.

Technical Challenges

  • Designing event models that support both replay and analysis.
  • Keeping query workflows fast enough for iterative research.
  • Normalizing messy external data into stable internal representations.
  • Making results inspectable instead of opaque aggregate outputs.

What This Demonstrates

Data modeling, SQL-heavy engineering, query optimization, analytics infrastructure, ETL, event-driven workflows, and the ability to design systems that turn raw data into useful operational decisions.
